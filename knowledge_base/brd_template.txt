A Business Requirement Document (BRD) should include:

1. Executive Summary
2. Business Objective
3. Project Scope
4. Stakeholders
5. Functional Requirements
6. Non-Functional Requirements
7. Assumptions
8. Constraints
9. Risks
10. Acceptance Criteria

Functional requirements must be clear, measurable, and actionable.
Non-functional requirements should include performance, security, scalability, and compliance.
